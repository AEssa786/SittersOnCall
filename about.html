About Sitters On Call

Sitters On Call was founded by a lifelong animal lover who believes pets are family.

Having spent a lifetime caring for animals of all kinds, including dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, goats, and various farm animals, I understand that every pet has its own personality, routine, and needs.

Unlike large pet care companies where your pet may be assigned to different sitters, I personally provide the care. This means pet owners know exactly who will be looking after their beloved companions while they are away.

Whether you're travelling for business, going on holiday, or simply need someone dependable to care for your pets, my goal is simple: to provide the same level of love, attention, and care that I would want for my own animals.

As an active supporter of animal welfare and the SPCA, animal wellbeing has always been close to my heart.

At Sitters On Call, your pets remain comfortable in their own home, following their normal routine, while receiving dedicated care from someone who genuinely loves animals.
