Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Still have questions? We've answered some of the things pet owners ask us most often.

Is the Meet & Greet really free?

+

Yes. Every new client receives a complimentary Meet & Greet where we get to know you, meet your pets, learn their routine, answer your questions, and make sure everyone feels comfortable before your booking begins.

Can you administer medication?

+

Absolutely. We have experience caring for pets requiring prescribed medication, specialised feeding routines, and extra care. We'll discuss your pet's individual needs during the Meet & Greet.

Will I receive updates while I'm away?

+

Yes. We provide regular updates, including photos, feeding confirmations, walks, medication given (if required), and anything else you'd like us to keep you informed about.

What happens in an emergency?

+

Before every booking we collect emergency contact details, your preferred veterinarian, backup contacts, medication information, and any special instructions so we're fully prepared should an unexpected situation arise.

Which areas do you cover?

+

We currently provide in-home pet sitting throughout Gauteng, including Johannesburg, Pretoria, and many surrounding suburbs. If you're unsure whether we travel to your area, simply get in touch and we'll be happy to help.

What if my plans change?

+

We understand that plans sometimes change. Please let us know as soon as possible and we'll do everything we can to accommodate changes or discuss any applicable cancellation terms.
